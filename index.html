About Sig Manufacturing

SIG has been a leading supplier to model airplane building and flying enthusiasts since 1951. Located in Montezuma, Iowa, SIG produces nearly 2,000 fine products for the model airplane hobby. Whether your airplanes fly by radio control (RC), control line (CL), or free flight (FF), SIG has what you need.
